What is Paper Trading?

Paper trading, also known as virtual trading or simulated trading, allows you to practice buying and selling stocks, options, or other financial instruments without using real money. It's an invaluable tool for beginners to learn the ropes of the market, test trading strategies, and understand market dynamics in a risk-free environment. Finnotech Pulse encourages responsible learning and offers resources to help you get started with paper trading platforms.
